## Information Collection and Use

### What We DO NOT Collect

Sabdaroopa does **NOT** collect, store, transmit, or process:

- Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
- Location data (GPS, Wi-Fi, cell tower)
- Device identifiers (IMEI, MAC address, advertising ID)
- Usage analytics or tracking data
- Crash reports or diagnostic data
- Search history
- Any data that leaves your device

### What Is Stored Locally on Your Device

The App stores the following data **ONLY on your device** (never transmitted to us or anyone else):

1. **Favorite Words**
    - Words you mark as favorites
    - Timestamp when favorite
    - Stored in local database

2. **Word Visit Statistics**
    - Number of times you view each word
    - Last visited timestamp
    - Used to show "recently viewed" suggestions

3. **App Preferences**
    - Theme selection (Light/Dark)
    - Font size preferences
    - Vibration settings
    - Language preferences

**Important:** All this data:
- Stays on YOUR device only
- Never leaves your device
- Is NOT transmitted to any server
- Is NOT shared with any third party
- Is NOT backed up to cloud
- Can be completely deleted by uninstalling the app

## Data Retention and Deletion

### How Long We Keep Data

**We don't keep any data** - all data stays on your device only.

### How to Delete Your Data

To delete all app data:

**Option 1: Clear App Data**
1. Go to your device Settings
2. Apps ‚Üí Sabdaroopa
3. Storage ‚Üí Clear Data

**Option 2: Uninstall the App**
