Information Collection and Use

What We DO NOT Collect

Sabdaroopa does NOT collect, store, transmit, or process:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data (GPS, Wi-Fi, cell tower)
  • Device identifiers (IMEI, MAC address, advertising ID)
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Crash reports or diagnostic data
  • Search history
  • Any data that leaves your device

What Is Stored Locally on Your Device

The App stores the following data ONLY on your device (never transmitted to us or anyone else):

  1. Favorite Words
    • Words you mark as favorites
    • Timestamp when favorite
    • Stored in local database
  2. Word Visit Statistics
    • Number of times you view each word
    • Last visited timestamp
    • Used to show "recently viewed" suggestions
  3. App Preferences
    • Theme selection (Light/Dark)
    • Font size preferences
    • Vibration settings
    • Language preferences

Important: All this data:

  • Stays on YOUR device only
  • Never leaves your device
  • Is NOT transmitted to any server
  • Is NOT shared with any third party
  • Is NOT backed up to cloud
  • Can be completely deleted by uninstalling the app

Internet Permissions

Sabdaroopa does NOT require or request internet permission.

The app functions 100% offline and cannot communicate over the network.

Permissions We Use:

  • Purpose: Provides haptic feedback when you interact with the app
  • Optional: Can be disabled in app settings

Permissions We DO NOT Use:

  • Internet/Network access
  • Location access
  • Camera access
  • Microphone access
  • Contacts access
  • Phone state access
  • Storage access (beyond app's own data)
  • Any other sensitive permissions

Third-Party Services

Sabdaroopa does NOT use any third-party services, including:

  • Analytics services (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
  • Advertising networks
  • Crash reporting tools
  • Cloud storage services
  • Social media integrations
  • Payment processors
  • Any external APIs or services

The app is completely self-contained with no external dependencies.

Data Security

Since no data is transmitted from your device:

  • Your data cannot be intercepted
  • No risk of data breaches from our servers (we don't have any)
  • No unauthorized access possible
  • Complete privacy by design

Local data is stored using Android's Room database with standard security practices.

Children's Privacy

Sabdaroopa is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13.

Why it's safe for children:

  • No personal information collected
  • No internet connectivity
  • No advertisements
  • No in-app purchases
  • No social features
  • No external links (except in-app About section)
  • Educational content only

We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children.

Data Retention and Deletion

How Long We Keep Data

We don't keep any data - all data stays on your device only.

How to Delete Your Data

To delete all app data:

Option 1: Clear App Data

  1. Go to your device Settings
  2. Apps ‚Üí Sabdaroopa
  3. Storage ‚Üí Clear Data
  4. This removes all favorites and preferences

Option 2: Uninstall the App

  1. Uninstalling the app completely removes all data
  2. No data remains on your device
